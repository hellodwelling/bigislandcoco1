Our Services

What we are best at

Tree Services

We know that keeping your landscape beautiful and green is a dynamic process. Some things come, some things go, and some things get cut back. Big Island Coconut Company Professional Tree Care & Landscaping provides tree services for every growth stage.

Tree Removal

Trees are a beautiful addition to any property, but what happens when they become hazardous? Due to dead limbs, high wind, or simple tree placement, what was once a thing of beauty can quickly become a threat to your home, cars, and property. We are here to help with our tree removal services. Our team will help you determine whether or not tree removal is justified and complete the job if so.

Shrub and Tree Trimming

Trimming can transform your property. Our experienced landscapers will rid your home or business of overgrowth and keep your shrubs and trees looking immaculate.

Patio Design and Installation

Patios can bring a yard to life with a place for the full family and guests to connect outside. We offer a variety of patio designs and each can be built custom.

Pavers and Walkways

Pavers can transform a yard from having now boundaries to being clearly organized and also help to prevent dead grass where the yard is commonly walked on.

Stone Wall Installation

Nothing makes for a better yard than the security and safety of a stone wall. With a wide selection and a team experienced installing all types of walls we are happy to help!
